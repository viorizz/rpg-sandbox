Reborn With Every Dawn

Dagny is a browser-based RPG where each sunrise reshapes the realm. Venture through ever-shifting dungeons and evolve your hero.

The Story

Long ago the sun vanished and the world fell into endless night. From that darkness emerged the first sunrise—an event known as the Dawning. The realm was reborn as Dagny, meaning both "day" and "new". Yet fragments of the old night persist as dungeons that erupt with every morning light. Only those who brave these shifting labyrinths can keep the world’s rebirth alive.

Four orders arose to defend the cycle of dawn: the berserkers of Solgard, the paladins of Dawntide Keep, the sorcerers of Nystara Sanctum, and the hunters of Wildsun Outpost. Their battles against the lingering bosses of night decide whether Dagny’s sunrise will continue.

Classes

Berserker

Forged in the blazing forges of Solgard, Berserkers channel raw solar fury through colossal weapons. Their rage turns the light itself into a weapon that breaks any foe.

Paladin

At Dawntide Keep, Paladins swear oaths to guard the newborn day. With shield and radiant might, they stand as unyielding bulwarks against the shadows.

Sorcerer

In the libraries of Nystara Sanctum, Sorcerers weave dawn’s first light into potent spells. They decipher the ancient runes that keep Dagny’s cycle turning.

Hunter

From the wild reaches near Wildsun Outpost, Hunters stalk creatures that slip between night and day. Their precision ensures no shadow escapes the morning.

The World of Dagny

  • Solgard – A volcanic village basked in perpetual sunrise, home of the Berserkers.
  • Dawntide Keep – A fortress that catches the first light, headquarters of the Paladins.
  • Nystara Sanctum – A crystalline city where Sorcerers study the runes of renewal.
  • Wildsun Outpost – A frontier settlement perched on the edge of forests and dunes, sheltering the Hunters.
